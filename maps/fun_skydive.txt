..: INSTALLATION :..
Just unzip this zip in your X:\Steam\SteamApps\account\counter-strike source\cstrike\ folder
Or manually put the bsp in the X:\Steam\SteamApps\account\counter-strike source\cstrike\maps\ folder and the
material file in the X:\Steam\SteamApps\account\counter-strike source\cstrike\materials\ folder
Then you're done

..: GAME PLAY :..
The T's and CT's starts out in tiny rooms. Each room got a button (and all buttons activates the floor).
The floor disappears and the players starts falling instantly.
The two shafts is approx 65324 units (~5443 feet , ~1660 meters).
The players fall thru this shaft, trying to avoid obsticles, hit ramps, hit teleports, and this at the same
time as killing the enemy.
The obsticles are yellow and black and the ramps are silver. The teleports are all highlighted with a green light
that's pulsating.

The default sv_gravity setting for CS is 800. Although I recommend something between 100-600 to be fine (depending
on what style of play u want. High grav = more skydiving. Low grav = more shooting), but 800 can be a lot of fun too.
