NOTES:  

Counter-terrorists have been constructing a fallout shelter.  The
building is designed to withstand a nuclear blast for many years, and
is currently under construction.  The counter-terrorists are funding
this project by buying and selling stolen paintings.  

OBJECTIVES:

Terrorists need to stop the funding for this project.  Destroy either 
storage rooms for the priceless paintings.  

Counter-terrorists must protect the paintings at all costs.  If they are
destroyed the fallout shelter cannot be completed.
