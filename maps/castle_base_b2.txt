+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
Step #1 - Installing the map
+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
Once your download is complete, right click the map and then click copy. Go to your maps folder (C:\Program Files\Steam\SteamApps\USERNAME\counter-strike source\cstrike\maps) then right click anywhere in the white and click paste from the right click menu. 

+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
Step #2 - Installing the textures (Needed inorder for map to function properly)
+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

Then go into your materials folder and put the sdt.vtf and sdt.vmt into the materials folder. Teh your ready to play!
